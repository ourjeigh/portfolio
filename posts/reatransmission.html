ReaTransmission

ReaTransmission streams audio directly from Reaper into the Wwise Authoring App or a game engine running Wwise, letting you hear your DAW audio in-game instantly! A Reaper extension captures the audio signal from the DAW, and a Wwise Source plugin controls Reaper playback and routes the Reaper signal though the Wwise engine. Once an instance of ReaTransmission is setup, sounds and FX can be added, removed, and modified in Reaper and all changes are heard live.
